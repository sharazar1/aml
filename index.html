Fatima Tariq

It's been two years since we were "us." Two years of learning how to live in a world that doesn't have you in it the way it used to.

And still — every single time, in the smallest, most unexpected moments, I miss you. Not the idea of you. You.

I don't know how time was supposed to fade that. Mine never got the memo. I still love you, quietly and completely, even from this distance we're standing at.

Today has nothing to do with any of that, though. Today is just yours. So happy birthday, Fatima — I hope it's soft, and full, and exactly as good as you deserve.

Some feelings don't need permission to stay. Mine didn't ask — it just did.
